Aldric

Also known as: The Hero, 勇者アルドリック, Anh hùng Aldric

Aldric, known as The Hero, is the protagonist of The Demon Lord's Duty. A graduate of both the Royal Academy of Heroes and the Clerk's Guild, he is unique among heroes for his deep belief that proper process is as important as justice itself.

Background

Early Life

Raised by two retired paladins — Ser Maren and Dame Elka — who ran a legal practice in the merchant quarter of Brighthollow. From an early age, Aldric showed equal aptitude for swordplay and legal scholarship.

Training

He graduated simultaneously from the Royal Academy (combat honors) and the Clerk's Guild (summa cum laude) — a feat never achieved before in the kingdom's history.

Personality

Aldric genuinely believes in justice and doing what's right — but he also believes deeply in process. To him, slaying the Demon Lord without proper authorization would be as wrong as the Demon Lord's crimes themselves.

Relationships

  • The Demon Lord — Rival turned reluctant friend, built over years of formal complaints and tea sessions.
  • Chamberlain — The Demon Lord's ancient servant who has grown to expect Aldric's visits.
  • Minister Thane — Political rival. stub

4. Key Design Decisions
Editor engine Reuses TiptapEditor with wiki-specific extensions (WikiLinkMark, SpoilerSection, InfoboxNode). Same tech stack as chapter editor. Data model Wiki articles stored as Tiptap JSON (like chapters). Linked to glossary entity via entity_id. Infobox fields come from glossary attribute_values. Wiki links [[Entity Name]] syntax in editor. Resolves to glossary entity. Blue = exists, red = stub/missing. Reuses GlossaryAutocomplete from P9-06. Templates Per entity kind. Writer can customize. Template defines default sections. New article auto-populates from template + glossary data. Spoiler protection Mark sections with /spoiler [chapter]. Readers see warning unless they've read past that chapter. Uses reading_progress data. Revisions Every edit creates a revision (like chapters). Full history with side-by-side diff. Restore any version. Community flow Community edits → suggestion queue → writer approves/rejects/edits. Already designed in screen-wiki.html section 5. AI assist Generate section from glossary data + chapter text. Uses reader's own API keys (BYOK). Cost transparency per operation. Cross-references "Pages that link here" panel (left sidebar Links tab). Shows which other wiki articles mention this entity. Media blocks Reuses chapter editor blocks (Image, Video, Audio) + new wiki-specific blocks: Gallery (multi-image grid with captions), Comparison (side-by-side before/after), Map/Diagram placeholder (SVG/PNG upload or AI generate). All captions support [[wiki links]]. Slash menu Type / in editor: /gallery, /compare, /map, /spoiler, /image, /video, /audio, /callout, /quote. Same slash menu as chapter editor, extended with wiki-specific commands. Auto-generate One-click generate wiki stub from glossary entity. Pre-fills infobox from attributes, generates article skeleton from template.
