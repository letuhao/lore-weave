Aldric, also known as The Hero, is the protagonist of The Demon Lord's Duty. He is the chosen hero of the Kingdom of Brighthollow, known for his unique approach to heroism that combines swordsmanship with bureaucratic expertise.

Background

Aldric was raised by retired paladins who ran a legal practice. He graduated from both the Royal Academy of Heroes and the Clerk's Guild simultaneously — a feat never before achieved in the kingdom's history.

His legendary sword "Precedent" has a filing system built into the pommel, which Aldric uses to keep track of legal precedents relevant to his current quest. He also carries a copy of the Hero-Demon Lord Engagement Protocol, a document he co-authored.

Personality

Aldric genuinely believes in justice and doing what's right — but he also believes deeply in process. To him, slaying the Demon Lord without proper authorization is as wrong as the Demon Lord's crimes themselves.

Relationships

  • The Demon Lord — Rival turned reluctant friend. Their relationship evolves from adversarial to a mutual respect built over years of formal complaints and tea sessions.
  • Chamberlain — The Demon Lord's ancient servant who has grown to expect Aldric's visits.
  • Minister Thane — A political rival in the kingdom who views Aldric's methods as weakness. stub
