The throne room was silent save for the crackle of dying embers. Shadows pooled in the corners where torchlight could not reach, and the air tasted of iron and betrayal. King Aldren sat motionless on his obsidian throne, his fingers gripping the armrests as he watched the doors at the far end of the hall.
