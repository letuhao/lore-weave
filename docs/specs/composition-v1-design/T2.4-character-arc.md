## 2. FE shape

- **hook** `features/composition/hooks/useCharacterArc.ts` вЂ” `useCharacterArc(projectId, entityId, token)` в†’ composes `listTimeline({entity_id})` + `getEntityDetail(entityId)` (reuse `features/knowledge/api.ts`). Pure `buildArc(events, beforeOrder)` selector.
- **component** `features/composition/components/CharacterArcView.tsx` (view) вЂ” the arc line (recharts/custom, shared with T2.3), the relations strip, the character picker (reuse `useCast`/roster). Extract `ArcEvent`.
- **api / types** вЂ” reuse `features/knowledge` (`listTimeline`, `getEntityDetail`, `Event`, `Relation`).
- **host** вЂ” a docked panel **or** a power-view; given it's per-entity and compact, lean a **docked sub-tab** that the Cast codex (T2.1) "view arc" action opens (Open Q4).

## 3. BE delta

**None new** вЂ” T2.1 builds the **status route** this track. Reuse `/timeline?entity_id=`, `/entities/{id}`, and the **new `GET /entities/{id}/status?before_order=`** (T2.1, `:EntityStatus` active|gone) for the state band. The arc spine is event-based; status adds the activeв†’gone band. *(REVIEW-IMPL HIGH-1: status, not `:Fact`.)*

## 4. Data contract

`GET /v1/knowledge/timeline?project_id=&entity_id=&before_order=&limit=` в†’ `{ events, total }` (the arc spine). `GET /v1/knowledge/entities/{entity_id}` в†’ relations (the strip). Spoiler cutoff = current chapter `event_order` (shared with T2.3).

## 5. Acceptance criteria

- Author picks a character and sees their events in narrative order, spoiler-safe.
- The current relations strip reflects 1-hop relations.
- Clicking an event opens its chapter; switching characters reloads the arc.
- No events for the character в†’ a clear empty state.
