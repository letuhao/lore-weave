The Gate of Ash rose out of the fog like a wound that had never closed. Kael felt the old cold settle into the empty place at his hip where a scabbard used to hang, and he made himself walk toward it anyway.

Mira was already there, of course. She always reached the dangerous places first. "You came," she said, and something in the word landed wrong — too pleased, too rehearsed.

"I came." He let his empty fingers fall open. "I have nothing left to draw," he said, "which is the only reason you should be afraid."✦ AI · unreviewed
